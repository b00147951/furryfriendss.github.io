Furry friends is a dog training and care center based in Co. Wicklow. We teach your dog good behavior, rehabilitation and improving socialization skills for all dog breeds.

We offer doggy services in luxurious surroundings that are sure to meet your expectations. Our services include dog training, dog grooming and a doggy hotel that will make your furry friend feel like they’re on vacation!

We understand that your dog is more than just a pet, they are a beloved member of your family. That’s why we treat every dog that comes through our doors with the same love and care that we give our own pets. So why settle for less when you can give your dog the best?

Choose Furry Friends for all your dog care needs, and discover the difference that true expertise and passion can make!
